TTDeck Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-30

TTDeck is a read-only vehicle monitoring app. It is not an official Tesla product, does not control vehicles, and is designed to connect to a Companion Server configured by the user.

1. Who We Are

TTDeck is provided by Fan Chentao ("we", "us", or "our"). You can contact us at xxxkkk0101@foxmail.com.

2. Summary

The current TTDeck app does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or cross-app tracking. The app stores connection settings on your device and uses a read-only token to request vehicle summaries from the Companion Server that you configure. Vehicle data is intended to remain in your TeslaMate installation, your Companion Server, your iPhone, and any infrastructure you choose to operate.

4. Companion Server and Self-Hosted Data

TTDeck depends on a Companion Server configured by you. That Companion Server may read from TeslaMate, Postgres, MQTT, Tesla services, DNS providers, and hosting providers selected by you. We do not control those services unless we separately provide and identify a developer-operated hosted service. Your use of those services is governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

5. Third Parties

TTDeck uses Apple platform services such as the App Store, iOS Keychain, local notifications, MapKit display, camera permission prompts, and iCloud Key-Value Storage for low-frequency settings sync. We are not responsible for data Apple collects independently through the App Store or iOS. If this policy is hosted on GitHub Pages, GitHub may process visitor information such as IP addresses for security and site operation.

6. Tracking, Advertising, and Sale of Data

TTDeck does not sell user data. The current app does not use third-party advertising SDKs, cross-app tracking, or third-party analytics SDKs. If we add analytics, crash reporting, remote push notifications, a hosted Companion service, or advertising in the future, we will update this policy and the App Store privacy information before releasing that version.

7. Retention and Deletion

Local app settings remain on your device until you change them, reset the app binding, or delete the app. The in-app reset action clears the read-only token and device secret from this device. Revoking server-side device access, deleting TeslaMate data, deleting Companion Server logs, and deleting hosted infrastructure data must be done on the server or service you control.

Support messages are kept only as long as reasonably needed to respond to your request, maintain records, or comply with legal obligations. To request deletion of support data you sent to us, contact xxxkkk0101@foxmail.com.

8. Your Choices

  • Use demo mode instead of connecting a Companion Server.
  • Change or remove the Companion Server URL and token in the app.
  • Use the app's privacy display options to hide exact location, route maps, VIN suffixes, place labels, and device token hashes on screen.
  • Disable local notifications in TTDeck or in iOS Settings.
  • Reset this device's binding from TTDeck Settings.
  • Revoke server-side device access from your Companion Server, where supported.

9. Children

TTDeck is not directed to children and should not be used by children to monitor vehicle or account data.

10. Security

TTDeck uses iOS Keychain for the read-only token and device secret. You are responsible for protecting your Companion Server, TeslaMate, database, setup secret, hosting account, and network configuration. Do not share raw tokens, setup secrets, Tesla tokens, database passwords, VINs, exact home coordinates, or full route payloads in public logs or support channels.

11. International Use

You are responsible for operating your Companion Server and connected services in compliance with laws that apply to you and your location.

12. Changes

We may update this policy when the app, data practices, or legal requirements change. The updated policy will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.

13. Contact

Questions, privacy requests, and support requests can be sent to xxxkkk0101@foxmail.com.
